Authentication

Beyond All Doubt

  • NGC / PCGS Graded — every coin certified by the world's leading independent numismatic grading services.
  • Certificate of Authenticity — issued by professional grading services and counter-signed by a qualified UK lawyer.
  • Unique Verification Code — like a Bitcoin TXID, each coin carries a traceable ID linked to its full history, metal composition, and year of origin.
  • Blockchain Record — authenticity and payment records stored on-chain via DLT for permanent, tamper-proof verification.
  • Secure Encapsulation — coins supplied in sealed, tamper-evident holders. Any breach is immediately apparent.
  • Legal Warranty — additional legal certification by a qualified solicitor provided with each purchase.
